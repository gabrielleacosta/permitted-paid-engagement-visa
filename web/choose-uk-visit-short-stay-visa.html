Choose a UK visit or short stay visa

Standard Visitor visa

Apply for this visa if you want to visit the UK on holiday or to see your family and friends, do business, take part in sports or creative events, do academic research or receive private medical treatment.

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No Yes (up to 30 days) Up to 6 months £93 (a long-term visit visa costs more)

Short-term study visa

Apply for this visa if you’re doing a short course of study or training in the UK.

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No Yes 6 or 11 months £97 for 6 months, £186 for 11 months

Visa to pass through the UK in transit

Apply for this visa if you need to pass through the UK on your way to another country.

How long you can stay and how much the visa costs depends on if you leave the airport and pass through UK border control.

If you leave the airport

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No No 48 hours £64

If you don’t leave the airport

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No No 24 hours £35

Permitted Paid Engagement visa

You might be able to apply for this visa if you’ve been invited to the UK as an expert in your profession.

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
Yes (if it’s related to your main job or area of expertise) No Up to 1 month £93

Marriage Visitor visa

Apply for this visa if you want to get married or register a civil partnership in the UK.

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No Yes (up to 30 days) Up to 6 months £93

Parent of a Tier 4 child visa

Apply for this visa if your child attends an independent fee-paying day school in the UK and you want to visit them.

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No No 6 or 12 months £516

Visit the UK in a Chinese tour group

Apply for this visa if you’re a Chinese citizen that’s coming to the UK as part of a tour group through the approved destination status agreement (ADS).

Can work Can study How long you can stay Cost
No No Up to 30 days £93
